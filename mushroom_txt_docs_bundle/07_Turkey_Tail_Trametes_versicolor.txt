Turkey Tail (Trametes versicolor) - Immune Modulation and Oncology Adjunct
Updated: 2025-10-10

Overview:
Rich in PSK and PSP polysaccharides, used alongside cancer therapies in Japan.

Mechanisms:
- Immune enhancement and modulation.
- Supportive role during chemotherapy/radiation.

Evidence Summary:
Clinical studies show immune support when combined with standard therapy.

Safety:
Generally safe; mild GI symptoms possible. Coordinate with oncologist before use.
