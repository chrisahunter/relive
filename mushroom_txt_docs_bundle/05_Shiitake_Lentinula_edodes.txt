Shiitake (Lentinula edodes) - Nutrient and Immune Support
Updated: 2025-10-10

Overview:
Culinary mushroom high in fiber and vitamin D. Contains lentinan, used as an adjunct therapy in oncology.

Mechanisms:
- Beta-glucans stimulate immune responses.
- Lentinan investigated for anticancer potential.

Evidence Summary:
Clinical use in Asia as adjuvant therapy; mixed outcomes.

Safety:
Possible dermatitis from raw shiitake; safe when cooked. Mild GI upset possible.
