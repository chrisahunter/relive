Lion's Mane (Hericium erinaceus) - Cognitive and Nerve Support
Updated: 2025-10-10

Overview:
Lion’s Mane is valued for its neurotrophic potential, affecting nerve growth factor pathways.

Mechanisms:
- Stimulation of nerve growth factor synthesis.
- Anti-inflammatory and antioxidant activity.
- Possible gut-brain axis modulation.

Evidence Summary:
Small studies show cognitive improvement in mild impairment, but not in healthy adults (2024 trial).

Safety:
Well tolerated; mild GI upset possible. Insufficient data for pregnancy.

Typical Use:
1–3 grams/day extract or powder. Choose verified fruiting body sources.
