Chaga (Inonotus obliquus) - Antioxidant and Immune Support
Updated: 2025-10-10

Overview:
Chaga is rich in melanin, polyphenols, and beta-glucans. Used for general immune and antioxidant support.

Mechanisms:
- Strong antioxidant and anti-inflammatory potential.
- Possible metabolic and antiviral effects (preclinical).

Evidence Summary:
Human data are limited; most findings are from animal studies.

Safety:
High oxalate content may stress kidneys with overuse. Avoid with anticoagulants or hypoglycemics.
