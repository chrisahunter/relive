Agaricus blazei (A. subrufescens) - Immune and Metabolic Support
Updated: 2025-10-10

Overview:
Contains beta-glucans and proteoglycans with immunomodulatory and metabolic activity.

Mechanisms:
- Supports immune function and antioxidant capacity.
- May aid glucose and lipid balance.

Evidence Summary:
Early clinical evidence; more research needed.

Safety:
Occasional liver enzyme elevations. Avoid with anticoagulants or hypoglycemics.
