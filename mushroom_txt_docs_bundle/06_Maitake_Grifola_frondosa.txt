Maitake (Grifola frondosa) - Immune and Metabolic Balance
Updated: 2025-10-10

Overview:
Contains D-fraction beta-glucans supporting immune and metabolic functions.

Mechanisms:
- Cytokine modulation.
- Blood glucose and lipid effects (early evidence).

Evidence Summary:
Small trials suggest immune activation and metabolic support.

Safety:
Well tolerated; possible hypoglycemia with antidiabetic drugs.
